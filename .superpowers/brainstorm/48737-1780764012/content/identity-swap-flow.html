Scoring — Which feels right?

🏆

Spies score for surviving

+3 per round a swap goes uncaught. +5 bonus if NO swaps caught. Incentivizes bold lying.

🔍

Detectives score for catching swaps

+3 for correctly identifying a swapped player. -1 for wrong accusations. Rewards careful observation.

⚖️

Both sides score simultaneously

Swapped players earn points for surviving. Truth-tellers earn points for catching. Everyone plays hard.
